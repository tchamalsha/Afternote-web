Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to PawVault. PawVault is a pet health vault app for dog owners, available on iOS and Android, built with React Native / Expo. It lets you store your dog's medical records, vaccination history, vet details, reminders, behaviour logs, and generate handover sheets for pet sitters.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. We are committed to keeping your information — and your pet's information — safe and private.

By using PawVault, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to provide the PawVault service:

Account Information

  • Your name and email address, collected via Firebase Authentication when you create an account or sign in.

Pet Profile Information

  • Dog name, breed, date of birth, gender, weight, and neutered status.
  • A pet photo — stored locally on your device only and never uploaded to any server.

Health & Care Records

  • Vet reports, medications, feeding schedules, and known allergies.
  • Vet clinic details and emergency contact information.
  • Vaccination history, reminders, and behaviour logs.

App Usage Data

  • Activity dates used to track your daily usage streak within the app. No detailed behavioural analytics are collected.
We do not collect location data, payment information, advertising identifiers, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect only to make PawVault work for you:

  • To create and manage your account securely.
  • To store, display, and sync your pet's health records and care information across your devices.
  • To send reminders you have set (e.g., vaccination due dates, medication schedules).
  • To generate handover sheets for pet sitters using your stored pet data.
  • To track your daily streak and display app activity within the app.
  • To respond to support requests you initiate.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your account data and health records are stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore, a cloud database. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections and stored with Firebase's built-in security controls.

Pet photos are stored locally on your device only. They are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party service.

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no system is completely immune to risk, and we encourage you to use a strong password and keep your device secure.

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data stored in Firebase is permanently deleted within 30 days.

5. Third-Party Services

PawVault uses the following third-party service:

  • Firebase (Google LLC) — used for user authentication (Firebase Auth) and cloud data storage (Firebase Firestore). Firebase may process your data on servers located outside your country. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

We do not use any advertising networks, analytics platforms (beyond Firebase's built-in infrastructure), or data brokers. We do not sell or share your data with any third parties for commercial purposes.

6. Children's Privacy

PawVault is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information without parental consent, please contact us at the email address below and we will delete that information promptly.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can view all data stored in your account directly within the app at any time.
  • Correction: You can update or correct any of your pet or account information from within the app.
  • Deletion: You can delete individual records, your entire pet profile, or your account at any time. Deleting your account permanently removes all associated data from our servers.
  • Data Portability: You may request a copy of your stored data by contacting us at the email below.
  • Withdraw Consent: You may stop using the app and request account deletion at any time.
